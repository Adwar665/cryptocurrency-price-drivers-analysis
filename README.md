# Econometric Analysis of Cryptocurrency Price Drivers

## Overview

This project investigates the determinants of cryptocurrency prices using a cross-sectional dataset of 60 cryptocurrencies from 2020.

The analysis combines cryptocurrency market data from CoinGecko with macroeconomic data from the Federal Reserve Economic Database (FRED).

## Research Question

Which factors most strongly influence cryptocurrency prices?

## Data Sources

### CoinGecko
- Cryptocurrency Prices
- Trading Volume
- Market Capitalization

### FRED
- U.S. 10-Year Treasury Rate

## Methodology

The study uses Ordinary Least Squares (OLS) regression analysis to evaluate the relationship between:

- Cryptocurrency Price
- Trading Volume
- Market Capitalization
- Volatility
- Interest Rates

## Key Findings

- Trading volume was the strongest statistically significant predictor of cryptocurrency prices.
- Market capitalization had limited explanatory power.
- Macroeconomic variables showed weaker effects than token-specific variables.
